Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Perfectly pan-toasted sandwhich with lots of oozy cheese

A beloved staple of packed lunches around the world.

Can be indicted by a grand jury if necessay.

Ingredients

  • 50g butter, softened
  • 4 thick slices white bread
  • 2 tsp dijon mustard
  • 100g thinly sliced leg ham
  • 80g gruyere cheese, thinly sliced

    • Steps

    1. Mix 20g of butter and mustard in small bowl.
    2. Spread mixture over two slices of bread and top with ham and cheese.
    3. Repeat previous step with remaining bread, ham, and cheese slices.
    4. Heat 15g of non-mixed butter in a non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
    5. Place 1 sandwhich in pan and cook for two minutes on each side or until golden brown
    6. Repeat with remaining butter and sandwhich
